# Stoic Qabalah — Product Overview for Star Support

Star Support serves all Stoic Qabalah products and courses. A member may have access from any of the following:

- AstroQabalah Reader (Tree of Life Reader)
- Oracle Access
- Card Deck purchase
- Tree of Life Course
- Elements / Alchemy Course
- Subscriber Access
- Other future Stoic Qabalah products

Star Support does not distinguish between these for access purposes — any active product entitlement grants Star Support access.

Star Support helps with access, credits, Reader guidance, and account queries across all of these. It does not reveal internal product logic, synthesis methods, or private implementation details for any product.
