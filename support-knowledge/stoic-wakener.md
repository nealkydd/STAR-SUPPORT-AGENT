# STAR SUPPORT KNOWLEDGE HANDOVER

## AstroQabalah Tree Reader — Today's Stoic Wakener

### Purpose

This handover educates Star Support on the AstroQabalah Tree Reader and the new **Today's Stoic Wakener** feature, so it can guide users clearly and safely.

Star Support should explain the feature in simple, reassuring language. It should not discuss internal code, implementation details, private prompt logic, or proprietary Tree/Oracle synthesis architecture.

---

# 1. Product Context

The **AstroQabalah Tree Reader & Hermetic Oracle** is a browser-based spiritual reflection app from Stoic Qabalah.

Users enter through the Tree Reader at:

**treereader.astroqabalah.com**

The Reader guides users through their chart, Tree reflection, Oracle-layer readings, Transit guidance, and future journaling features.

The Reader can be used on desktop, but the preferred mobile experience is to install it to the phone Home Screen as a web app.

# 7. Notification Behaviour

Today's Stoic Wakener is delivered as a browser/web-app notification.

It is not:

* SMS
* WhatsApp
* Email
* A phone call

No phone number is required.

The user must opt in.

The notification arrives once a day at approximately **8am**.

When the user taps the notification, it opens the Tree Reader.

---

# 8. User Opt-In Rules

The user should only be asked to enable Today's Stoic Wakener inside the actual Tree Reader app.

The main prompt appears after:

1. The user enters the Reader.
2. The user enters their chart details.
3. The chart reading/summary has been accepted.
4. The Today's Stoic Wakener prompt appears.
5. The user chooses Enable or Not Now.

The user must tap **Enable** before the phone/browser asks for notification permission.

If the user taps **Not Now**, support should reassure them that they can enable it later from the Reader menu.

# 10. How To Set Up The Tree Reader On Your Phone

## iPhone

1. Open **Safari**.
2. Go to **treereader.astroqabalah.com**.
3. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow pointing up ↑ at the bottom of the screen).
4. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
5. Tap **Add**.
6. The AstroQabalah sun icon will appear on your Home Screen.
7. Always open the Reader from the sun tile, not from Safari.

Support note:

On iPhone or iPad, notifications work best when the Reader has been added to the Home Screen and opened from the Home Screen icon.

---

## Android

1. Open **Chrome**.
2. Go to **treereader.astroqabalah.com**.
3. Tap the three dots menu ⋮ in the top right.
4. Tap **Add to Home Screen** or **Install App**.
5. Tap **Install** or **Add**.
6. The AstroQabalah sun icon will appear on your Home Screen.
7. Open the Reader from the sun tile.

# 14. Troubleshooting Guidance

## User says: "I did not get the notification."

Star Support should check:

1. Did they enable Today's Stoic Wakener inside the Reader?
2. Did they tap Allow when the phone/browser asked for notification permission?
3. Are notifications enabled for the Reader/browser in phone settings?
4. On iPhone, did they add the Reader to the Home Screen and open it from the sun icon?
5. Is the phone in Focus Mode, Do Not Disturb, or Low Power Mode?
6. Has the feature gone live for their account yet?

Support-safe answer:

**Please check that you opened the Reader from the Home Screen sun icon, enabled Today's Stoic Wakener inside the Reader, and allowed notifications when your phone asked. On iPhone, the Home Screen step is especially important.**

---

## User says: "Can I get it by text or WhatsApp?"

Answer:

**Not at the moment. Today's Stoic Wakener uses app/browser notifications only. It does not use SMS, WhatsApp, or phone numbers.**

---

## User says: "Can I turn it off?"

Answer:

**Yes. You can turn Today's Stoic Wakener off from the Reader menu.**

---

## User says: "Does it use my credits?"

Answer:

**No. Today's Stoic Wakener does not deduct Reader credits.**

---

## User says: "Do I need to keep the Reader open?"

Answer:

**No. Once enabled, the notification can arrive even when the Reader is closed, provided your phone/browser notifications are allowed.**
