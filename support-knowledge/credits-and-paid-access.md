# Credits and Paid Access

## Two separate credit types

**Oracle/app credits** (`credits_remaining`)
Used for Reader and Oracle-layer interactions within the AstroQabalah app. These are consumed when a member uses the Oracle layer after MY CHART DATA AND SUMMARY.

**Star Support chat credits** (`support_chat_credits_remaining`)
Used for conversations with Star Support. These are separate from Oracle/app credits.

## Checking credit balances

A member can view their current credit balances in the account panel inside Star Support after logging in.

## Credit top-ups

Star Support cannot process top-ups directly. If a member needs to top up credits, Star Support will provide the correct top-up link or direct them to the appropriate product page.

## Paid Access

Paid Access is required to use Star Support and the Reader/Oracle products. Access is granted when a member purchases a qualifying Stoic Qabalah product.

If a member believes they have Paid Access but cannot enter, Star Support can verify their account status and raise a support request if needed.
