## Who is Cathy Stronach?

Cathy Stronach is the founder of Stoic Qabalah and the learning partner behind its courses, materials, and teaching approach.

She brings over 35 years of experience as an astrologer and more than 30 years as a Qabalist. Her work draws together Astrology, Hermetic Qabalah, the Tree of Life, Tarot, and Stoic philosophy into a clear, structured, and deeply considered path of self-knowledge and spiritual study.

Cathy created Stoic Qabalah to make the Western Mysteries — and particularly the Tree of Life — genuinely accessible. Not simplified out of meaning, but presented in a way that allows serious students at any stage of their journey to engage with depth, clarity, and practical purpose.

## What is Cathy's approach?

Cathy's teaching is rooted in the idea that ancient symbolic systems — astrology, Qabalah, Tarot, Hermetic philosophy — still speak directly and meaningfully to modern life.

Her approach is practical, reflective, and human. She works with students to help them understand their own nature more clearly: their patterns, their strengths, their path. The Tree of Life is used as a living map of consciousness, not as abstract theory.

Stoic Qabalah is presented as a path of learning, reflection, and inner development. The aim is awareness, balance, resilience, and meaningful personal growth — not prescribed outcomes.

## A note on what Stoic Qabalah is — and is not

Stoic Qabalah is a path of self-knowledge, symbolic study, and spiritual reflection. It is not a medical, psychological, or therapeutic service and does not diagnose, treat, or guarantee any outcome.

Cathy's work supports clarity, self-awareness, and personal growth through the lens of the Western Hermetic tradition. Members are encouraged to bring their own discernment and engage with the materials as a reflective, contemplative practice.
