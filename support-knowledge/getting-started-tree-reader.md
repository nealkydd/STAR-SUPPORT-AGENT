## How to start the Tree of Life Reader

To begin, you work through MY CHART DATA AND SUMMARY. This is the entry stage that opens the full Reader and Oracle layer.

**Step 1 — Enter your birth date**
Use your day, month, and year of birth.

**Step 2 — Enter your birth time**
Use the most accurate time you have. If you are unsure, use your closest available information. An approximate time is better than none.

**Step 3 — Enter your birth place**
Enter the town or city and country where you were born.

**Step 4 — Review your chart summary**
The Reader creates your personal chart summary from the details you have entered.

**Step 5 — Continue into the Reader**
Once MY CHART DATA AND SUMMARY is complete, the main Reader area opens. The Oracle layer becomes available after this stage.

---

## If you are unsure of your birth time

An exact birth time gives the most precise chart. If you do not know it, use the closest time available — morning, afternoon, or evening — where the Reader allows. You can note that the time is approximate.

For official birth records, a birth certificate is the most reliable source.

---

## What you need before you begin

- Your birth date (day, month, year)
- Your birth time (as accurate as possible)
- Your birth place (town/city and country)

That is all that is needed to begin. The Reader guides you through the rest.

---

## After MY CHART DATA AND SUMMARY

Once this stage is complete, the main Reader area opens and the Oracle layer becomes available. If you reach this stage and the Oracle is not appearing, check that MY CHART DATA AND SUMMARY is fully complete — and let Star Support know if you need help.
