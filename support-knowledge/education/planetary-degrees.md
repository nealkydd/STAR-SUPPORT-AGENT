## What are planetary degrees?

Each of the twelve zodiac signs spans 30 degrees of the sky, giving a full circle of 360 degrees. The degree of a planet describes its precise position within a sign — from 0° (just entered the sign) to 29° (at the very end, approaching the next sign).

Degrees add nuance to astrological interpretation. A planet at 0° of a sign is in a very different symbolic position from the same planet at 29° — and certain degrees carry traditional or symbolic significance in their own right.

---

## Early, middle, and late degrees

**0°–3° — Early degrees**
A planet here is often understood as being at the threshold — the energy of the sign is fresh, unformed, and full of potential but not yet fully expressed. There may be an element of initiation or new beginning associated with these positions.

**4°–26° — Middle degrees**
The planet is fully within the sign's territory. The qualities of the sign are expressed with more stability and confidence. The middle degrees are often considered the most settled expression of a sign's energy.

**27°–29° — Late degrees (anaretic)**
The final degrees of a sign — sometimes called the anaretic degree (particularly 29°) — are traditionally associated with urgency, completion, or a sense of things coming to a close before a transition. A planet here may be understood as carrying the full weight of a sign's experience, preparing to move on.

## Degree symbols

Two systems of symbolic degree meanings are commonly used in astrology:

**The Sabian Symbols**
A set of 360 symbolic images — one for each degree of the zodiac — channelled in 1925 by Elsie Wheeler and organised by astrologer Marc Edmund Jones. Each degree has a short evocative image (e.g. "A man turning his back on his passions and teaching from his experience"). They are used as a meditative or reflective tool — not as literal prescriptions.

**The Charubel and other degree systems**
Various historical systems assign symbolic keywords or images to degrees. These differ between traditions and are generally used for reflective inquiry rather than definitive interpretation.

## What does a specific degree mean?

When someone asks about a specific degree — for example, "What does 15 degrees of Aries mean?" — the answer involves several layers:

- **The sign quality**: Aries is a Fire/Cardinal sign, associated with initiative, courage, and new beginnings
- **The degree position**: 15° is the middle of Aries — fully into the sign's energy
- **The Sabian Symbol**: 15° Aries carries the image "An Indian weaving a blanket" — traditionally associated with the patient, skilful creation of something that will endure
- **Any planets present**: If a planet falls at this degree in a birth chart, its symbolic meaning combines with the sign and degree qualities

These layers are used together for reflective study and interpretation. They are symbolic reference points, not fixed meanings.
