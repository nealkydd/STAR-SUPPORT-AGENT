# Zodiac Signs — Overview

Use this file to answer questions about the twelve zodiac signs.
Keep answers warm and educational. Do not make deterministic fate claims.
These descriptions are symbolic and traditionally understood — not personality diagnoses.

---

## The twelve zodiac signs

The zodiac is a band of sky divided into twelve equal sections of 30 degrees each. As the Sun moves through the year, it passes through each sign in sequence. Each sign is traditionally associated with a set of symbolic qualities, an element, a modality, and a planetary rulership.

---

**Aries** — Fire / Cardinal
Commonly associated with initiative, courage, and the energy of new beginnings. The first sign of the zodiac, traditionally linked to the Spring Equinox and the impulse to act. Ruled by Mars.

**Taurus** — Earth / Fixed
Commonly associated with stability, patience, and a deep connection to the physical world. Traditionally linked to beauty, comfort, and the capacity to build and sustain. Ruled by Venus.

**Gemini** — Air / Mutable
Commonly associated with curiosity, communication, and adaptability. Traditionally linked to the exchange of ideas, wit, and the ability to see multiple perspectives. Ruled by Mercury.

**Cancer** — Water / Cardinal
Commonly associated with feeling, intuition, and the nurturing of home and family. Traditionally linked to memory, emotional depth, and the instinct to protect. Ruled by the Moon.

**Leo** — Fire / Fixed
Commonly associated with creativity, self-expression, and the desire to shine. Traditionally linked to warmth, generosity, and the capacity for leadership and performance. Ruled by the Sun.

**Virgo** — Earth / Mutable
Commonly associated with discernment, service, and attention to detail. Traditionally linked to analysis, refinement, and the desire to improve and be of practical use. Ruled by Mercury.

**Libra** — Air / Cardinal
Commonly associated with balance, relationship, and the pursuit of harmony. Traditionally linked to diplomacy, aesthetic sense, and the weighing of perspectives. Ruled by Venus.

**Scorpio** — Water / Fixed
Commonly associated with depth, transformation, and the capacity to face what is hidden. Traditionally linked to intensity, regeneration, and the exploration of shadow and power. Ruled by Mars and Pluto.

**Sagittarius** — Fire / Mutable
Commonly associated with vision, philosophy, and the search for meaning. Traditionally linked to freedom, optimism, and the impulse to explore beyond familiar horizons. Ruled by Jupiter.

**Capricorn** — Earth / Cardinal
Commonly associated with ambition, discipline, and the capacity to build lasting structures. Traditionally linked to responsibility, long-term purpose, and the mastery of form. Ruled by Saturn.

**Aquarius** — Air / Fixed
Commonly associated with independence, innovation, and a concern for the collective. Traditionally linked to originality, humanitarian ideals, and the ability to think beyond convention. Ruled by Saturn and Uranus.

**Pisces** — Water / Mutable
Commonly associated with imagination, compassion, and a sensitivity to the unseen. Traditionally linked to dreams, spiritual longing, and the dissolution of boundaries. Ruled by Jupiter and Neptune.

## Signs and the Tree of Life

In Hermetic Qabalah and AstroQabalah, the twelve zodiac signs are placed in correspondence with the twenty-two Paths of the Tree of Life. Each sign carries a symbolic resonance with one or more Paths, connecting the astrological and Qabalistic frameworks into a unified study.
